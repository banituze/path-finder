Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the PathFinder assessment? +

PathFinder is designed to provide guidance based on your interests and strengths. While no assessment is perfect, it gives you a solid starting point for exploring specialisations that align with your preferences.

Can I retake the assessment? +

Absolutely! You can retake the assessment as many times as you'd like. Your interests may evolve as you learn more about each specialisation.

Is my data stored anywhere? +

No, PathFinder runs entirely in your browser. Your responses are not stored on any server, they're processed locally and your results are generated client-side.

Who created PathFinder? +

PathFinder was created by Winebald Banituze, a BSE student at the African Leadership College of Higher Education, as a tool to help fellow students navigate their tech journey.
